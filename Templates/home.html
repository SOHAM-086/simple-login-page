Welcome to the Indian Cricket team's Page

The Indian Cricket Team represents India in international cricket. It is governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). The team is one of the most successful in the world, having won numerous prestigious tournaments, including the ICC Cricket World Cup. India has a passionate cricket fanbase and has produced many legendary cricketers such as Sachin Tendulkar, MS Dhoni, and Virat Kohli. The Indian cricket team continues to inspire millions with their dedication and sportsmanship.
